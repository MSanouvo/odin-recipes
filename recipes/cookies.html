Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, using a hand or standing mixer, beat butter and sugars to cream. Make sure to beat for about 2-3 minutes. Once creamed, add in egg and vanilla and beat to incorporate.
  3. Shift mixture to the side and add flour, salt, and baking soda. Gently mix the dry ingredients together. Using a spoon or you mixer, stir the dry ingredients into wet to fully incorporate, scraping down the sides of the bowl if needed. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Use a large cookie scoop to spoon dough or roll into 8-10 even balls (about 2-3 inch balls) onto parchment lined or silicone lined baking sheet. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until edges become slightly browned.
  5. Store in an airtight container. Sprinkle with flaked sea salt if desired.
